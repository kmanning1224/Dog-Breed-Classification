Our technology doesn’t just help purebred pups, it also allows rescues & owners to better predict what possible breed(s) of dog they’re giving a “Furr-ever Home” to. While only genetic testing can give an exact match/ mix, this machine learning technology will give an idea of what superficial characteristics could mean breed-wise, helping promote an “Adopt don't Shop” ideal.
